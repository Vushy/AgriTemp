About the Temperature and Humidity Forecasting

The agriculture industry faces many challenges, especially when it comes to predicting weather conditions. Unpredictable temperature and humidity can severely impact crop growth, leading to lower yields and wasted resources. Accurate weather forecasting is crucial for farmers to plan their activities properly and minimize risks.

Our system, AgriTemp, provides real-time weather updates for temperature and humidity that are accessible through web, mobile and desktop platforms. This helps farmers make informed decisions and take necessary actions to protect their crops, improving their productivity and sustainability.
